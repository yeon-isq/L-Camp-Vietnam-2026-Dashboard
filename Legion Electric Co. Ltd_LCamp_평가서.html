ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
LegionOS addresses all three dimensions of Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) performance. Environmentally, the platform reduces food waste and energy consumption through AI-driven workflow optimisation and real-time quality control. Socially, it improves workplace safety by automating repetitive and hazardous tasks while enabling employees to focus on higher-value customer service and operational roles. From a governance perspective, LegionOS provides real-time operational visibility, standardised processes, and data-driven decision-making, improving compliance, traceability, and accountability across restaurant operations. A proof-of-concept collaboration with LOTTERIA and LOTTE Innovate would enable us to quantify these impacts in a real-world environment by measuring reductions in food waste, operational inefficiencies, and quality deviations. Successful validation would create a scalable framework for supporting LOTTE's sustainability, digital transformation, and operational excellence objectives across its food service businesses.
