ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
Agritree Vietnam addresses key ESG challenges by promoting sustainable agriculture, distributed renewable energy, and climate resilience through Solar Sharing. Solar Sharing technology reduces greenhouse gas emissions and supports efficient land use without requiring additional land development. Solar Sharing enhances farmers’ income diversification, strengthens rural energy resilience, and supports sustainable food production. From a governance perspective, the project contributes to science-based decision-making and future policy development through collaboration with research institutions and stakeholders. PoC collaboration with LOTTE will accelerate these impacts by demonstrating low-carbon and climate-resilient agricultural supply chains, supporting Scope 3 decarbonization, and creating sustainable sourcing and branding opportunities within LOTTE’s ecosystem. The collaboration would provide a scalable model for ESG-driven food systems across Vietnam and ASEAN.
