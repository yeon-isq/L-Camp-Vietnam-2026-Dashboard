Social (S) Impact: Fairness, Diversity, and Development The solution's greatest impact is in the "Social" pillar, specifically supporting LOTTE's "Creating a Great Workplace" and "Social Value Creation" strategies. - Promoting Diversity and Inclusion (D&I): The AI engine scores candidates based on objective behavioral data (empathy, problem-solving, compliance) rather than academic background or age. This directly supports LOTTE’s Diversity Charter and its commitment to avoiding discrimination based on gender, generation, or nationality. - Enhancing "Spec-tackle" Recruitment: By digitizing LOTTE’s unique blind recruitment system, the solution ensures that "only the person" is seen throughout the process, focusing purely on job skills. - Upskilling and Reskilling: The platform facilitates LOTTE’s Digital Transformation (DT) goals by providing adaptive training scenarios for front-line workers in Retail and F&B, helping employees acquire new skills (reskilling) or improve existing ones (upskilling) through simulated practice. - Reducing Attrition: A "Competency Passport" helps employees track their growth and allows managers to intervene before burnout or churn occurs, fostering a more stable and satisfied workforce. 2. Environmental (E) Impact: Carbon Footprint Reduction Digitalization of L-TAB: Moving the L-TAB (personality and aptitude test) and training sessions to a 100% online-only simulation format eliminates the need for candidates and employees to travel to offline testing centers or classrooms. This significantly reduces the carbon footprint associated with recruitment and training logistics >>> Net Zero Target
