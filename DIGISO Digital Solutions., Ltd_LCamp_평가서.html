ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
DIGISO’s LOTTE Omni-Signal Growth App addresses ESG impact by helping LOTTE shift from mass marketing to more responsible, data-driven, and customer-relevant engagement. Environmentally, the solution can reduce inefficient promotions, unnecessary voucher waste, overstock risk, and poorly targeted demand spikes by using enriched customer signals, campaign simulation, and next-best-action recommendations before campaigns are launched. Socially, it improves customer experience by sending more relevant offers to families, healthy food seekers, K-culture consumers, dormant customers, and loyalty members, while also supporting digital inclusion through DIGISO’s proven AI training and adoption experience. A PoC with LOTTE could create this impact by using infrastructure from LOTTE Mart, LOTTERIA, LOTTE Cinema, and LOTTE Innovate to test targeted campaigns such as Family Weekend Combo, Healthy Basket, Cinema-to-F&B, and Dormant Customer Win-back. The solution would measure ESG-aligned business outcomes such as higher campaign efficiency, reduced irrelevant outreach, improved repeat engagement, smarter cross-sell, better customer satisfaction, and more transparent campaign performance through dashboards and human-in-the-loop approval. From a governance perspective, the PoC supports responsible AI deployment through secure data handling, human approval before activation, measurable KPI tracking, and auditable AI-assisted marketing decisions.
