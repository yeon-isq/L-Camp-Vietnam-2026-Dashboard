VNETWORK's solutions address ESG impact across all three dimensions: Environmental (E) — Reducing Waste and Logistics Emissions Through AI Optimization VNETWORK's most direct and measurable environmental impact comes from AI-driven optimization of the distribution chain. Our AI-powered Smart Distribution platform reduces over-ordering, excess and near-expiry inventory, and unnecessary delivery trips through accurate demand forecasting and optimized routing. Fewer wasted goods and fewer redundant logistics journeys translate directly into lower material waste and reduced transport-related emissions across LOTTE's retail and supply chain operations. A PoC with LOTTE would make this impact measurable: by tracking inventory turnover, waste reduction, and delivery route efficiency on covered systems, VNETWORK can provide LOTTE with quantifiable data to support its own ESG reporting and green transition targets concrete operational metrics rather than unverifiable carbon claims. Social (S) — Digital Safety & Inclusion Cyberattacks disproportionately harm consumers exposing personal data, disrupting essential services, and eroding trust in digital platforms. VNETWORK's VNIS and EG-Platform protect the personal data and digital transactions of millions of LOTTE's end customers across retail, hospitality, logistics, and F&B. By securing LOTTE's digital ecosystem, the PoC directly contributes to a safer digital environment for Vietnamese consumers and employees. Additionally, VNCDN's content acceleration technology ensures equitable access to LOTTE's digital services for users across all regions of Vietnam, including underserved areas with limited connectivity. Governance (G) — Regulatory Compliance & Data Protection Vietnam's regulatory landscape is rapidly evolving, with stringent requirements around cybersecurity and data protection (Decree 356/2025/ND-CP on Personal Data Protection, Decree 85/2016/ND-CP on IT System Security, Decree 15/2020/ND-CP on administrative penalties). VNETWORK's solutions are fully licensed by Vietnam's Ministry of Information and Communications (License 121/GP-BTTTT) and certified to ISO 27001 and ISO 20000-1 international standards. A PoC collaboration with LOTTE would help LOTTE's Vietnam affiliates achieve and demonstrate full regulatory compliance, reducing legal risk and strengthening corporate governance across the group's local operations. How a PoC with LOTTE amplifies this ESG impact: By integrating VNETWORK's solutions across LOTTE's diverse business units from LOTTE Mart and LOTTE Mall to LOTTE Hotel and LOTTE Global Logistics the PoC creates a scalable model of responsible digital transformation that can be replicated across LOTTE's broader Asia-Pacific operations. This collaboration positions both VNETWORK and LOTTE as leaders in sustainable, secure, and socially responsible digital innovation in Vietnam.
