ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
Yeast Era addresses several important Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) challenges through sustainable biomass fermentation technology. **Environmental Impact** Our yeast-based protein production process requires significantly fewer natural resources compared to conventional animal protein production. The technology utilizes biomass fermentation to produce high-quality protein with lower land use, lower water consumption, and reduced greenhouse gas emissions. The platform also supports circular economy principles by utilizing agricultural raw materials and improving resource efficiency. **Social Impact** Yeast Era aims to improve access to affordable, highly digestible, and functional nutrition. Our products provide protein, beta-glucan, and soluble fiber that support healthy aging, metabolic health, immune health, and nutritional well-being. This is particularly relevant for aging populations and individuals seeking healthier nutrition options. **Governance Impact** Yeast Era is building a transparent and traceable food production system supported by quality management processes, product traceability, regulatory compliance, and intellectual property protection. We are committed to responsible innovation and food safety standards. **How a LOTTE PoC can create ESG impact** A PoC collaboration with LOTTE Mart and other LOTTE affiliates would allow Yeast Era to validate and scale sustainable nutrition solutions through a trusted retail ecosystem. The pilot would help increase consumer awareness of sustainable protein, improve access to healthier food choices, generate measurable consumer and environmental impact data, and accelerate the adoption of innovative nutrition products in Vietnam. Through LOTTE's retail, hospitality, and digital ecosystem, the partnership could demonstrate how sustainable food innovation can create both business value and positive ESG outcomes at scale.
