ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
As a social-impact agriculture enterprise, EcoNations addresses Environmental impact through ecological, regenerative farming that helps restore soil, water and ecosystem balance and reduces chemical use; Social impact by helping farmers earn a fair, stable living on their own land and giving consumers safe, transparent, traceable produce; and Governance through full farm-to-table traceability and transparent value-chain management. A PoC with LOTTE would amplify this impact by bringing clean, traceable ecological products to LOTTE's large retail and F&B customer base, scaling demand for sustainable agriculture, expanding income for participating farmers, and demonstrating a transparent, responsible supply chain at commercial scale.
