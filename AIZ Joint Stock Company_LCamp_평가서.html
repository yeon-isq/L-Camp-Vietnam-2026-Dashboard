ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
ESG Impact & How a Lotte PoC Accelerates It: AIZ BrainOS addresses ESG across all three dimensions — not as a reporting layer, but as a structural outcome of operational AI deployment. Environmental. Lotteria's current order fragmentation across five platforms creates direct waste: kitchens over-prepare during peak hours due to unpredictable order flow, incorrect orders generate food waste and redundant delivery trips, and SLA breaches trigger re-dispatches that multiply transportation emissions. Our Orchestration Agent attacks this at the root — by unifying and predicting order flow in real time, it reduces kitchen overproduction, cuts error-driven food waste, and eliminates unnecessary re-routing. These are not theoretical gains; they are measurable at store level from Month 2 onward. Scaled to 270+ Lotteria stores, the aggregate reduction in food waste and delivery inefficiency becomes a reportable environmental metric for Lotte Group's ESG disclosures. Social. AIZ employs a fully Vietnamese engineering team, and every wave of Lotte collaboration creates additional skilled AI jobs within Vietnam — directly advancing the KOICA IBS initiative's goal of building local technology capacity rather than importing foreign solutions. At the store level, the agent reduces repetitive, high-stress manual work (toggling between five tablets during peak hours) and frees staff to focus on customer-facing service, improving both working conditions and job satisfaction. Beyond Lotteria, Waves 3–4 deliver deeper social impact: behavioral credit-scoring through Lotte Finance expands financial access for thin-file consumers (young workers, gig economy participants), and EHS vision agents at Lotte Chemical and E&C directly reduce workplace injury rates through real-time safety monitoring. Governance. Data sovereignty is central to BrainOS architecture. All Lotteria data is processed within Vietnam, with optional on-premise deployment via AIBOX edge devices and full audit logging — meeting Korean enterprise IT governance standards without relying on third-party cloud providers holding sensitive operational data offshore. AIZ Chairman Vũ Thanh Thắng has publicly contributed to Vietnam's data sovereignty policy discourse, and our Qualcomm ISV partnership provides independent validation of our technology governance standards. For Lotte, this means a PoC partner that treats compliance as architecture, not afterthought. Why the Lotte PoC specifically amplifies this impact. Lotte Group's unique structure in Vietnam — retail, F&B, finance, chemical, and construction under one umbrella — means a successful PoC at Lotteria does not stay isolated. It becomes the data and operational foundation for ESG gains across the entire Lotte Vietnam ecosystem: reduced waste in retail (Wave 2), financial inclusion through Lotte Finance (Wave 3), and industrial safety at Lotte Chemical and E&C (Wave 4). No single-vertical AI startup can generate this compounding ESG trajectory. The L-CAMP PoC is the catalyst that sets all four waves in motion.
