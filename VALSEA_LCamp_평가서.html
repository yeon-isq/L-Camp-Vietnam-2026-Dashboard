VALSEA's solution advances the Social dimension of ESG through accessibility and inclusion: accurate speech recognition for accented, multilingual, and code-switched Vietnamese means customers who are underserved by standard English-trained AI — including older, rural, and dialect-speaking populations — are understood and served equitably. It also supports digital language inclusion, helping preserve and properly process Southeast Asian languages that global systems handle poorly. On Governance, full-call transcription and structured records improve transparency, auditability, and compliance in customer interactions versus today's 2% manual sampling. A PoC with LOTTE would demonstrate this at scale: more equitable customer service across LOTTE's diverse Vietnamese customer base, with auditable, transparent interaction records.
