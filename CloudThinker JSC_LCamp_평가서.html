ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
Cloud and DevOps work carries heavy on-call loads and a constant stream of operational toil that drives burnout, attrition, and lost productivity. Our AgenticOps model — "engineers on the loop, not in the loop" — hands routine detection, remediation, and infrastructure work to autonomous agents. This does two things: it returns night-and-weekend hours to people, and it frees LOTTE's development teams from undifferentiated ops work so they can focus on building product. The result is measurably higher developer throughput and the ability to run expert-level operations with leaner, healthier teams. The PoC lets LOTTE Innovate quantify both the reduction in after-hours burden and the gain in development-team productivity.
