ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
The impact we address: the frontline worker is the most under-invested asset in industrial Asia. Enterprise software serves managers and analysts; the people who physically move goods get paper, memory and pressure. Minder's ESG case is built on changing that, with effects that are measurable rather than declarative. Social. Vietnamese logistics workers are often young, in their first formal job, and fluent in neither Korean nor English. Minder gives each of them an expert voice in their own language that trains them on the job, keeps their eyes up and hands free around forklifts and in sub-zero rooms, and turns safety checklists into spoken routines that actually get completed. The result is faster competence, fewer accidents, lower stress, higher retention, and genuinely more skilled and promotable workers. It also removes the language hierarchy inside a Korean-Vietnamese operation: knowledge moves down without workers needing Korean, and floor reality moves up without managers needing Vietnamese. Environmental. In a cold chain, every handling error is spoilage, and spoiled chilled food carries its full upstream carbon footprint plus disposal. Guided first-time-right execution reduces waste directly. Faster, guided receiving and disciplined door and zone handling reduce door-open time and cold air loss, which is one of the larger refrigeration energy drains in cold storage. Digital voice-driven logs also remove paper from daily operations. Governance. Every task, temperature check and critical control point becomes a timestamped, attributable digital record, giving HACCP-grade traceability and audit readiness by default rather than by paper sampling. For a facility handling fresh food at scale, including volumes from Lotte's agricultural partnerships, this is food safety governance that protects the Lotte brand. Privacy is engineered in: voice is processed on the device itself, so workers' speech never leaves the floor, and client operational data sits in strictly isolated tenants. How a PoC with Lotte creates the impact: the PoC converts each of these from intention into a published number from a live Lotte facility, namely measured reductions in waste, errors, accidents, onboarding time and energy-relevant door time, plus a complete digital compliance trail. Those numbers become reportable cases for Lotte's sustainability commitments and, through Lotte Innovate, a template the group can replicate across Vietnam and beyond. The impact compounds with every site, because the same Knowledge Port that guides Dong Nai's workers can guide every frontline team Lotte operates. ESG here is not a programme bolted onto the business; it is what the product does when it works.
