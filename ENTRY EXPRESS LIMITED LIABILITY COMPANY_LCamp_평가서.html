Idea

Entry Express는 베트남 관광지 및 이벤트장을 위한 디지털 티켓팅 SaaS이다. 종이 티켓 기반 수동 운영으로 매출 누수와 긴 대기줄을 겪는 현지 중소형 관광 시설 문제를 해결하며, 월 500매 이하 무료 티켓 수수료 + 구독료 + 키오스크 임대료($120/월)로 운영된다.

⚠ 핵심 미검증 가정 오프라인 우선 아키텍처가 실제 베트남 인터넷 불안정 환경에서 작동하며, 월 500매 초과 티켓을 판매하는 중소형 관광지들이 유료 전환할 것이라는 가정이 검증되지 않았다.

ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
- Environmental — Digital ticketing eliminates printed paper tickets and physical receipts at scale. A single mid-size venue processing 1,000+ tickets/month can eliminate significant paper waste annually once fully digitized. Our offline-first design also reduces dependency on constant data infrastructure, lowering the energy footprint compared to cloud-only alternatives. - Vietnam's tourism venues are heavily concentrated in major cities, leaving smaller venues and emerging destinations — particularly in rural and remote areas — without access to modern ticketing infrastructure due to cost, technical barriers, and unreliable internet connectivity. Entry Express offline-first architecture solves this directly: the platform operates fully without internet access, requiring only a mobile phone to sell, scan, and manage tickets. This makes digital ticketing viable even in areas with no stable network coverage — a common reality for many natural parks, heritage sites, and rural attractions across Vietnam. Combined with tiered pricing (starting free for under 500 tickets/month), Entry Express makes digital transformation accessible to venues that larger enterprise platforms — which assume constant connectivity — simply cannot serve. - Governance — Our real-time analytics dashboard gives venue operators (including Lotte) full visibility into ticket sales, revenue, and visitor data that is often informally or manually tracked today — reducing opportunities for revenue leakage and improving financial transparency for venue management. How could a PoC collaboration with LOTTE help create that impact? A successful pilot at LOTTE Cinema and LOTTE Hotel would demonstrate that digital ticketing infrastructure works within a major corporate ecosystem — creating a credible reference case that smaller, resource-constrained venues across Vietnam can point to when adopting similar technology. Lotte's validation would accelerate trust-building in a market where digital transformation in tourism still lags behind other sectors.
