ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
MMM addresses ESG impact by helping multi-site businesses reduce electricity, water and gas waste through real-time monitoring, AI anomaly detection and actionable utility insights. From an environmental perspective, MMM helps reduce unnecessary energy consumption, water leakage and gas inefficiency, which can contribute to lower CO₂ emissions and better resource efficiency. From a social perspective, the solution supports safer, more reliable facility operations by detecting abnormal utility usage earlier, reducing operational risks and improving the working environment for staff and customers. From a governance perspective, MMM provides transparent, traceable and data-driven utility reports that can support ESG reporting, internal audits, ISO 50001 energy management initiatives and sustainability decision-making. A PoC collaboration with LOTTE would help create measurable ESG impact by applying MMM to real multi-site operations such as LOTTE Mart, Lotteria, LOTTE Cinema, LOTTE Hotel or LOTTE Mall. During the PoC, MMM can establish baseline electricity, water and gas consumption, detect abnormal usage, estimate saving opportunities, and generate ESG-ready reports showing potential reductions in energy use, water waste and CO₂ emissions. If validated, the model can be scaled across LOTTE’s wider ecosystem in Vietnam, turning sustainability from a reporting activity into practical daily operational improvement.
