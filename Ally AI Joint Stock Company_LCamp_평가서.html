워케이션 시장은 성장 가능성이 있으나, 베트남 내 한국인 워케이션 시장 규모(SAM/SOM)에 대한 구체적 데이터가 전혀 제시되지 않음 [미확인]. 1,000+ 사용자 주장은 국적·유료 전환율·재방문율 정보가 없어 시장 검증 수준을 판단할 수 없음 [미확인]. '외국인 전문가'라는 타겟이 지나치게 광범위하며, 한국 시장 특화 전략이 실제 수요에 기반한 것인지 불명확. 경쟁사(Airbnb 장기숙박, Selina, Outsite 등)와의 차별화 전략이 구체적으로 설명되지 않음 [미확인]. 확장 가능성은 롯데 인프라 활용 시나리오에만 의존하며 독립적 성장 경로가 부재.

### ESG Impact TripC addresses both social and environmental challenges associated with the future of work, tourism, and international mobility. **Social Impact (S)** Many international professionals, entrepreneurs, and remote workers face significant barriers when relocating to a new country, including social isolation, lack of trusted information, language barriers, and limited access to local communities. TripC helps people integrate into local society more effectively by providing trusted accommodation, coworking spaces, community programs, cultural experiences, and multilingual local support. Our platform encourages meaningful connections between international visitors and local communities, creating opportunities for cultural exchange, knowledge sharing, entrepreneurship, and economic participation. For local businesses, TripC also creates new opportunities by connecting international residents with verified local service providers, restaurants, tourism operators, wellness businesses, and cultural experiences. **Environmental Impact (E)** TripC promotes longer-stay and lower-impact travel compared to traditional short-term tourism. By encouraging workcation and long-stay living models, travelers generate fewer transportation emissions per day of stay while contributing more sustainably to local economies. The platform also encourages the use of local services, walkable neighborhoods, coworking spaces, and community-based experiences rather than high-frequency tourist activities with higher environmental footprints. **Governance Impact (G)** TripC builds trust through verified service providers, transparent information, community standards, and responsible recommendations. Our goal is to reduce misinformation and improve access to reliable local services for international residents while supporting responsible tourism development. ### Potential ESG Impact Through a PoC with LOTTE A PoC collaboration with LOTTE Hospitality could demonstrate how hospitality infrastructure can evolve beyond traditional accommodation into a sustainable living and work ecosystem for international residents. Together, LOTTE and TripC could: * Create one of the first structured Workcation ecosystems for Korean professionals in Vietnam. * Support cultural exchange and community integration between Korean residents and local communities. * Increase utilization of existing hospitality assets through longer-stay, lower-impact travel models. * Generate economic opportunities for local SMEs, restaurants, tourism providers, and service businesses. * Develop a scalable ESG-focused model that could be expanded to other LOTTE destinations across Southeast Asia. The long-term vision is to build a more inclusive, sustainable, and community-driven model of international living, working, and travel.
