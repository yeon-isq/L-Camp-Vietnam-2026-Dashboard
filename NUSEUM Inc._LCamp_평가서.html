ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
NUSEUM addresses ESG impact by helping transform everyday food decisions into scalable preventive healthcare, healthier consumption, and more inclusive access to clinical-grade nutrition intelligence. From a social impact perspective, NUSEUM supports SDG 3, Good Health and Well-being, by helping reduce preventable chronic disease risk through personalized, evidence-based food guidance. This is especially relevant in Vietnam, where diabetes, hypertension, dyslipidemia, obesity, and overweight conditions are rising. By embedding nutrition intelligence into retail and digital food journeys, NUSEUM can help consumers make safer daily choices before health problems become hospital issues. From an inclusion perspective, NUSEUM supports SDG 10, Reduced Inequalities, by making clinical nutrition intelligence accessible outside hospitals and specialist clinics. A LOTTE PoC could bring health-aware food guidance to ordinary consumers through LOTTE Mart Vietnam, digital commerce, hotels, and future senior-living or community channels. From an environmental and responsible consumption perspective, NUSEUM supports SDG 12, Responsible Consumption and Production, by guiding consumers toward healthier and more intentional food choices. Over time, AI-guided food commerce can help LOTTE promote better product discovery, reduce misaligned purchases, support healthier food ecosystems, and connect commerce with long-term public health outcomes. A PoC collaboration with LOTTE would help create this impact by using LOTTE’s retail, hospitality, food, and digital infrastructure as a real-world deployment environment. Starting with LOTTE Mart Vietnam, the PoC could test health-aware smart cart recommendations, condition-aware product guidance, and safer food alternatives for consumers managing diabetes, hypertension, obesity, allergies, or medication-related food risks. LOTTE Hotels & Resorts could also test health-aware menu guidance for guests. The 3–6 month PoC would generate measurable ESG outcomes, including consumer engagement with healthier recommendations, repeat usage, satisfaction, healthier basket shifts, and feasibility for scaling clinical-grade nutrition AI across LOTTE’s ecosystem. If successful, the collaboration could position LOTTE as a leader in preventive-health commerce in Vietnam and create a scalable model for healthier, more responsible food decisions across APAC.
