On The Cloud는 베트남 5성급 호텔(LOTTE Hotel L7, InterContinental, Sheraton 등)에서 프리미엄 웨딩 및 프라이빗 이벤트를 위한 맞춤형 공간 디자인·연출·운영 서비스를 제공하는 기업이다. 고객은 웨딩 주최자이며, 수익 구조는 프로젝트당 디자인·기획·실행 일괄 서비스 수수료로 운영된다.

ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
Social & cultural: OTC's work preserves and modernizes Vietnamese ceremonial culture — including traditional wedding rituals — bringing heritage into contemporary, elevated experiences. Partnering with LOTTE would let this cultural-experience standard reach a far wider audience through LOTTE's venues. Environmental: Premium events are often highly wasteful (single-use décor, floral waste). Through this PoC, OTC would pilot a more sustainable event model — reusable and modular design elements, reduced material waste, and responsibly sourced materials — using LOTTE's recurring venue calendar as the ideal setting to standardize and scale these practices. Governance: Moving from one-off vendor jobs to a documented, repeatable operating standard improves transparency, quality control, and accountability — a model LOTTE can audit and replicate across affiliates.
