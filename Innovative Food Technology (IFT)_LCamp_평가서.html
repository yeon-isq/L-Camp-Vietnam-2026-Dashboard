ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
ESG impact addressed by Lemit Lemit addresses ESG impact by transforming underutilized Mekong young jackfruit into low-carbon plant-based and blended/hybrid meat ingredients. Environmentally, our solution supports meat-reduction pathways, circular use of agricultural biomass, lower food waste, and future product-level carbon measurement through traceability and MRV/LCA. Socially, Lemit creates new value for local jackfruit farmers and rural supply chains by turning an underused crop into higher-value food ingredients. Lemit currently works with more than 30 farming households and purchases young jackfruit at approximately 20–25% higher than the prevailing market price, helping improve farmer income, create more stable demand, and reduce the risk of agricultural waste. From a governance perspective, Lemit aims to build a more transparent and measurable supply chain through traceability, food safety standards, ESG data, and responsible sourcing. How a PoC with LOTTE can help create impact A PoC collaboration with LOTTE can turn this ESG potential into measurable business evidence. Through LOTTE Mart, LOTTERIA, LOTTE Hotel and/or LOTTE Global Logistics, Lemit can test real customer acceptance, operational feasibility, sustainable menu or retail concepts, and supply-chain traceability in a corporate environment. If successful, the PoC can create a practical model for low-carbon food innovation in Vietnam: young jackfruit sourced from more than 30 local farming households at fairer prices, processed into structured ingredients, tested through LOTTE’s retail, foodservice and hospitality channels, and potentially scaled into wider B2B supply. This would support LOTTE’s ESG goals by offering healthier, lower-carbon, locally sourced food options while creating rural value and a traceable circular food supply chain.
