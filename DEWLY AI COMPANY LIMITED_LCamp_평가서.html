ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
- Social: Dewly is designed to augment, not replace, hospitality staff. By automating repetitive coordination and offering 24/7 multilingual support, it reduces burnout and after-hours pressure on front-line teams, lets small operators access an "operations manager" capability they otherwise couldn't afford, and improves service accessibility for international guests through Vietnamese, English, and Korean support. - Governance: The platform is built around transparency and human-in-the-loop control owners and managers retain full oversight, with every automated action logged and auditable, and complex or sensitive cases escalated to staff. This brings consistency, accountability, and clearer SOP enforcement to fragmented hospitality operations. - Environmental: By optimizing housekeeping schedules, maintenance dispatch, and turnover timing, Dewly cuts unnecessary trips, idle energy use, and resource waste across properties—supporting more efficient, lower-footprint operations. How a LOTTE PoC creates impact: Piloting at L7 West Lake's serviced residences would let us measure these effects at scale quantifying staff hours saved and reduced overtime (Social), demonstrating auditable, owner-controlled AI governance in a real enterprise setting (Governance), and tracking efficiency gains in housekeeping and energy use (Environmental). The results would form a replicable ESG model that LOTTE could extend across its hospitality and residential portfolio in Vietnam.
