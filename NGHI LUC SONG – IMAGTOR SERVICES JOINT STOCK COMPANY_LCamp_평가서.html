"Imagtor's core impact is Social: sustainable employment for vulnerable people. Imagtor is a social enterprise co-founded and chaired by Nguyễn Thị Vân — a person with disability and a BBC 100 Women 2019 honoree. Today ~35% of our staff are people with disabilities and 48% are women (83% of senior management roles held by women), directly advancing SDGs 8, 10 and 5. Our shareholder MYSC (a Korean impact investor) and partner KOICA validate that this impact is real. Standardizing product images is easy to train people with disabilities for, so every volume of work from LOTTE directly creates new jobs (we can onboard ~50 more people/year). LOTTE provides the scale (20,000+ SKUs), so the social impact grows with order volume. This is also ESG-to-ESG: it helps LOTTE Mart strengthen its Social pillar in a measurable way."
