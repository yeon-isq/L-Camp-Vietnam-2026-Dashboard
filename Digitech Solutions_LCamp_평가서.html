ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
DigiAI Platform creates ESG impact by helping businesses transform manual operating processes through AI-powered digital workers and AI Agents. From an environmental perspective, the platform helps businesses reduce reliance on administrative staff for repetitive tasks and partially replace manual execution with AI digital workers. This enables companies to operate more leanly, reduce paper-based processes, minimize manual work, optimize resources, and build a more efficient digital workplace. From a social perspective, DigiAI Platform improves workplace quality by reducing repetitive administrative workload and allowing employees to focus on higher-value tasks such as customer service, problem-solving, business development, and decision-making. From a governance perspective, the platform centralizes business data, improves workflow transparency, supports task and KPI tracking, and enables role-based access control for better management and accountability. A PoC collaboration with LOTTE could demonstrate these ESG impacts across retail, e-commerce, logistics, customer service, and internal operations. Measurable outcomes may include reduced manual workload, faster customer response time, lower paper usage, better workflow visibility, improved employee productivity, and more data-driven decision-making.

'통합 플랫폼'이라는 포지셔닝은 Odoo, Zoho, 로컬 ERP 솔루션 대비 기능적·가격적 차별점이 불명확 [미확인]. 30개 고객사의 산업 분포, 도입 계기, 기존 솔루션 대체 여부 등 Go-to-Market 전략의 실증적 근거 부족.

'컨설팅 + SaaS' 결합이 기술 기반 확장성보다 노동집약적 서비스에 의존할 위험.
