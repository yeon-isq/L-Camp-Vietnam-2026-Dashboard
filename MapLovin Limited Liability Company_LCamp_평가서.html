MapLovin supports ESG by helping local businesses and retail tenants improve digital accessibility, customer responsiveness, and service quality. More accurate Google Maps information can reduce wasted visits caused by wrong addresses, hours, or duplicated pins. Review intelligence and QR feedback help businesses detect customer pain points earlier and respond more responsibly. A PoC with LOTTE can create impact by improving store-level customer experience, supporting tenants and SMEs with better digital visibility, and helping retail operators make more data-informed service improvements.
