ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
Athsent addresses ESG primarily through the Social and Governance dimensions. On the Social side, the platform helps reduce workplace accidents, improve worker protection, and strengthen safety culture in high-risk environments such as construction and industrial operations. On the Governance side, Athsent helps organizations improve HSE compliance, maintain better visibility over site conditions, and create structured safety data for monitoring, reporting, and accountability. A PoC collaboration with LOTTE would create impact by demonstrating how AI-enabled safety monitoring can move construction safety from manual supervision to proactive, data-driven risk management. It would also help validate a scalable model for safer construction operations in Vietnam, with potential long-term benefits for workers, contractors, project owners, and broader industry standards.
